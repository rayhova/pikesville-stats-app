## App Store Listing

Promotional text:

```text
Private team command center for Pikesville basketball players, coaches, managers, and administrators.
```

Description:

```text
Pikesville MBB is the private team app for the Pikesville basketball program.

The app gives invited players, coaches, managers, and administrators one place to manage the team workflow during the season.

Players can view their schedule, RSVP for team events, complete assignments, receive alerts, review development plans, and access approved team resources.

Coaches can prepare for practices and games, review assignments, track attendance, add scouting notes, view reports, create development plans, and support live game operations.

Administrators can manage rosters, seasons, practices, games, scouting, assignments, alerts, staff access, and program reporting.

Key features include:

- Team schedule and RSVP tracking
- Practice plans and drill library
- Assignments with proof review
- Player profiles and development plans
- Scouting reports, game plans, and timeout cards
- Live game scoring workflow
- Team alerts and reminders
- Coach, manager, player, and admin role-based access

Pikesville MBB is not open for public registration. Access requires an invite from the Pikesville basketball program.
```

## Submission Click Path

1. App Store Connect > My Apps > Pikesville MBB.
2. TestFlight > confirm latest build processed.
3. Distribution > iOS App > prepare version `1.0`.
4. Add screenshots.
5. Paste subtitle, description, keywords, support URL, privacy URL, and review notes from this document.
6. Complete App Privacy using the draft above.
7. Complete age rating.
8. Complete encryption with "None of the algorithms mentioned above."
9. Add demo login.
10. Submit for review.
